About legacy Server

Legacy Server is a prominent Organization in the Aftermarket Android side of the world.

We Help create great things, support multiple ROM organizations and offer our services of Linux Servers, Gerrit hosts, and Jenkins providers for dozens of people all over the globe

About Legacy Team

We are the Team of normal people around the diffrent corners of Earth. Android brought us together and we formed the "Legacy Team".

Legacy Team had a passion and love for Android and they started to contribute the community in one or anotherway.

Team decided to help the beginners who want to learn about Android Compliation and ROM Cooking but they cannot start due to their low Desktop/Laptop Specifications and slow or low Bandwidth of Internet.

We brought up the Legacy Servers for the Community with the best services available in market.
